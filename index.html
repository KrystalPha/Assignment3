    • PROFILE

    YOASOBI is a Japanese music duo consisting of Ayase, a producer and songwriter, and Ikura (real name: Lilas Ikuta), a vocalist. They debuted in 2019 with the viral hit "Yoru ni Kakeru," which quickly became a chart-topping success in Japan. YOASOBI is known for blending pop, electronic, and J-pop elements with storytelling through their lyrics, often inspired by short stories or novels.
